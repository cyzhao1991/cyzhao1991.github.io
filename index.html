Scholar

I am a Research Fellow at the School of Computer Science and Statistics, Trinity College Dublin.

My research interests include:

  • Autonomous decision making systems
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Agentic AI
  • Engineering applications of machine learning

I have an MEng in Engineering Science and a DPhil (PhD), both from the University of Oxford. My DPhil was on "Visual recognition in Art using Machine Learning" with Andrew Zisserman in the VGG group. After my DPhil, I was a postdoc at the School of Informatics in Edinburgh with Amos Storkey.

I hold an EPSRC New Investigator Award. I am an investigator on the dAIEdge Horizon Network and a co-investigator on the EPSRC AI Hub for Causality in Healthcare AI.

I am Programme Manager for Electronics and Electrical Engineering at Edinburgh and I have written a machine learning course.
